Our Mission:

Kind Hands is a website that was created to inform our audience on the issues of poverty in developing nations around the world. We focus on countries in the continents of Africa, Central/South America, and Asia. We are passionate about this because poverty is a huge problem that is overlooked by many people and countries. Kind Hands educates our audience through true stories of actual people who live in poverty. We also have a game to educate our younger audiences the harsh realities these people live through everyday. We also partner with multiple organizations to provide help to those living in poverty. We ensure that our donations go directly to fighting poverty one kind hand at a time.
